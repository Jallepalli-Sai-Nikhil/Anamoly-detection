Google Stocks Anomaly Detection

Welcome to the Google Stocks Anomaly Detection tool! In this project, we apply three powerful anomaly detection methods to identify unusual patterns in Google's stock data:

  • Isolation Forest: A tree-based model that isolates anomalies instead of profiling normal data points.
  • LOF (Local Outlier Factor): A density-based method that identifies anomalies based on local density deviation.
  • DBSCAN (Density-Based Spatial Clustering of Applications with Noise): A clustering-based method that detects outliers as points that do not belong to any cluster.

Once you upload the data, the plots for the anomalies detected by each of the three methods will be generated and displayed below.
